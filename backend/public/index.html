HOW WE KEEP IT THAT WAY

Good Ingredients Deserve
a Container That Doesn't Cheat Them

Everything we make is kept in borosilicate glass — the same glass used in labs and hospitals. It doesn't leach chemicals, doesn't absorb odours, and doesn't quietly age your food the way plastic does. It's not the star of the show. It's just how we make sure the star stays exactly as good as the day we packed it.

THE ROZVIK™ STORY

Good Food, Made
Without Shortcuts.

ROZVIK™ started with a simple standard: if we wouldn't feed it to our own family without checking the label twice, we wouldn't sell it.

That's the whole idea. Every almond is hand-picked, every ladoo is rolled by hand in small batches, every chocolate is set the same week it's ordered — not sitting in a warehouse for months. No shortcuts, no fillers, no "natural flavour" doing the work real ingredients should.

We keep it all in reusable glass, because good food deserves a container as honest as what's inside it. But the glass was never the point. The food is.

Divyesh Kaushik
FOUNDER, ROZVIK™
"Good food doesn't need shortcuts. Ours doesn't have any."
